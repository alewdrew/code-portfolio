Our Founder & Head Trainer

Meet Miranda

Miranda is the founder and head trainer of Multi-Sport K9, LLC. Since a young age, Miranda has always been passionate about animals, especially dogs. She decided to pursue a career to specialize in dog training. ​ Miranda is the youngest person on record to attend the Tom Rose School of Professional and Master Dog Training where she studied under top trainers. After obtaining her Certification in Professional Dog Training she worked as the head dog trainer and training manager at Yuppy Puppy Pet Spa. Miranda trained hundreds of dogs on the basics to advanced obedience skills, including a facility dog for United Services for Children with disabilities. Miranda is a certified AKC Trick Dog and Canine Good Citizens Evaluator. ​ She is also a board member of Dogs That Help, an organization that provides veterans and others in need with service dogs to gain more independence and improve their quality of life. Training philosophy: Miranda believes in balanced training methods & all positive methods using the 3 pillars of dog training: timing motivation consistency ​ Her goal with all her clients is to teach them how to communicate effectively with their dog(s) and get the most out of their relationship. Miranda looks forward to helping you with all your training needs!
