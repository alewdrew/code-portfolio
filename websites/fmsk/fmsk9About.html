Meet The Trainers

Mirand Matuszewski

Founder & Lead Trainer

Miranda has always had a passion for animals, primarily dogs, from a young age. Her passion led her to explore careers in the dog industry, where she discovered training was her niche. Miranda studied Professional and Master Dog Training courses with honors in 2017. After obtaining her Certification in Professional Dog Training , Canine Good Citizens, & Trick Dog evaluators license she worked at Yuppy Puppy Pet Spa as the head dog trainer and training manager, training hundreds of dogs from basics to advanced obedience including a facility therapy dog for United Services for children with disabilities. Miranda has 3 dogs. Gunner her 12-year-old Siberian Husky who is Certified in Canine Good Citizens, trained in Rally, advanced off leash obedience, & therapy dog work. Ryker her 2-year old Dutch Shepherd who competes in protection, weight pulling, and certified in Canine Good Citizens. He is trained in advanced off leash obedience, scent work, & is soon competing for his PDC in PSA and Dock Diving Titles. Rambo her 4-year-old Chocolate Labrador Retriever is trained in special retrieves, IPO tracking, IPO 3 obedience, Trick Dog, Protection work, scent work, Agility, therapy dog, Disc, Dock Diving, Canine Good Citizens, Rally, & has many titles in AKC including Urban Canine Good Citizen, Rally Advanced, Advanced Trick Dog, Open, & competed in Rally Nationals this past year finishing 11th out of hundreds competing with a score of 199/200. Rambo is currently working towards his Rally Excellence, Trick dog Performer, Utility title.. Miranda believes in balanced training methods as well as all positive methods using the 3 pillars of dog training, which are timing, motivation, & consistency. Her goal with all her clients is to teach them how to communicate effectively with their dog(s) and get the most out of their relationship. Miranda looks forward to helping you with all your training needs!
